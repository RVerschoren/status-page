HPC-UGent Tier2 job schedulers paused

Start: 2023-03-14 13:00:00
End: Unknown
Updated: 2023-03-14 14:06:34
Level: medium

Due to a stability problem with the HPC-UGent shared filesystems, we have paused the job schedulers for all HPC-UGent Tier-2 clusters. You can still submit jobs to the queue, but they will not start until the job schedulers are resumed. While job schedulers are paused, the status of jobs will appear as 'F' in the output of the qstat command (because they can not get started currently). Jobs that were running already may have crashed due to instability problems with the shared filesystems ($VSC_DATA, $VSC_SCRATCH, ...). We are working on resolving the problems ASAP.

Update Tue 14 March 2023 - 15:00

Schedulers are getting resumed.
